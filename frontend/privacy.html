Privacy Policy

BalanceWhiz is built around operational trust. This page summarizes what we collect and why—in plain language, not legalese wallpaper.

What we collect

  • Account details you provide (for example, name and email).
  • Forecast and household data you enter in the product (bills, income, balances, and related notes).
  • Technical data needed to run the service (for example, authentication cookies, basic logs, and support messages you send us).

What we do not do

BalanceWhiz does not require linking your bank. We do not sell your personal data to data brokers, and we do not use it for unrelated advertising profiles.

How we use information

To operate your account, provide the forecast, respond to support requests, meet legal obligations, and protect security and integrity of the service.

Retention

We keep information as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for backups, disputes, and legal requirements.

Contact

Questions or requests: Contact Us or email support@balancewhiz.com.
